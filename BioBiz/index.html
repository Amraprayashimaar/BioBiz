About BioBiz

At BioBiz, we are keen to capitalize on India’s rich biodiversity and the
availability of large amounts of biomass residues to develop a
sustainable bio-economy and attractive business opportunities for
diverse stakeholders – farmers, rural stakeholders, end-use industries,
entrepreneurs / startups, and financial investors.

As part of a leading climate tech consulting firm, we will provide critical
assistance with market intelligence, data-driven analyses, professional
networking, and opportunities for relevant stakeholders to get effective
visibility.
